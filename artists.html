Fivio Foreign

Fivio Foreign

Top Accomplishments:

Breakout Single: "Big Drip" (2019)

Collaborations with: Alicia Keys, Lil Tjay

Pioneering the Drill Scene in Brooklyn

Favorite Songs: "City of Gods", "For Nothin", "Through the Fire"

Maxie Lee Ryles III, known professionally as Fivio Foreign, is an American rapper and songwriter from Brooklyn, New York. Born on March 29, 1996, Fivio gained widespread recognition with his breakout single "Big Drip" in 2019. You could argue that Fivio has been THE artist to bring drill to mainstream culture. After Pop Smoke passed a couple years back, Fivio took over as the king of New York and has been carrying the drill industry on his back since. He has been known for his catchy ad-libs and embodies drill music.

Central Cee, born on December 4, 1998, is a British rapper and songwriter from Shepherd's Bush, London. His tracks often reflect on his experiences growing up in West London, addressing societal issues and personal struggles. Cench is arguably the current biggest artist in the UK. Coming from nothing, his hard work, lyrical rap, and smooth flows have helped him to earn his current success. As an ambassador of the UK, Cench is a leader in shedding light on UK drill in the US.

Lil Tjay

Lil Tjay

Debut Album: "True 2 Myself" (2019)

Hit Single: "F.N"

Collaborations with: Polo G, Pop Smoke

Favorite Songs: "Beat the Odds", "Project Walls", "Goin' Up"

Tione Jayden Merritt, known professionally as Lil Tjay, is an American rapper and singer from the Bronx, New York. Born on April 30, 2001, Lil Tjay gained widespread recognition with his melodic style and introspective lyrics. Lil Tjay was famously shot six times and survived the hits. He brings a soul to his music and speaks on his survival, mental health, and his upbringing. He is one of the realest artists you will find.
